Explore Birmingham's Diverse Entertainment Destinations

The Hippodrome

Iconic theater hosting world-class performances, from musicals to ballet, in a grand historic setting.

The Hippodrome

Discover the iconic Birmingham Hippodrome, a cultural gem in the heart of the city, offering world-class performances and captivating entertainment. As a cornerstone of Birmingham's arts scene, the Hippodrome invites visitors to experience the magic of live theater and dance. Learn more about upcoming shows and events on the Birmingham Hippodrome website.

Back To Back Houses

Historic dwellings showcasing Birmingham's industrial past, offering insight into working-class life in compact living spaces.

Back to Back Houses

Explore Birmingham's back-to-back houses, a cherished piece of history maintained by the National Trust. These humble abodes offer a window into the city's industrial past and the lives of its working-class residents. Visit the National Trust website to learn more.

Resorts World

Vibrant entertainment complex featuring shopping, dining, gaming, and leisure activities all under one roof.

Resorts World Arena

Experience the excitement of live events and entertainment at Resorts World Arena in Birmingham. From electrifying concerts to thrilling sporting events, this premier venue offers unforgettable experiences for visitors of all ages. Check out the latest schedule and book your tickets on the Resorts World Arena website for an unforgettable outing.

Bear Grylls Adventure

Thrilling destination offering adrenaline-fueled experiences, from indoor skydiving to high ropes challenges.

Bear Grylls Adventure

Embark on an exhilarating adventure at The Bear Grylls Adventure in Birmingham. Dive into action-packed challenges inspired by the renowned adventurer himself, from indoor skydiving to high ropes courses and survival mazes. Whether you're seeking adrenaline-fueled thrills or family-friendly fun, The Bear Grylls Adventure offers an unforgettable experience for all. Explore the adventure options and book your expedition on The Bear Grylls Adventure website.

Symphony Hall

Premier concert venue renowned for its world-class acoustics, hosting orchestral performances, concerts, and cultural events.

The National Sea Life Centre

Fascinating aquatic attraction showcasing diverse marine life, educational exhibits, and interactive experiences for all ages.

National Sea Life Centre

Dive into a world of wonder at the National Sea Life Centre Birmingham. Explore captivating marine habitats and encounter a dazzling array of underwater creatures, from majestic sharks to playful sea otters. With interactive exhibits, educational presentations, and immersive experiences, the Sea Life Centre offers a fascinating journey into the depths of the ocean. Discover the magic of marine life and plan your visit today by exploring the National Sea Life Centre Birmingham website.

Birmingham Museum and art Gallery

Cultural treasure trove housing diverse exhibits, artworks, and artifacts, offering insights into local and global history.

Cadbury World

Deliciously immersive attraction offering chocolate-themed tours, interactive experiences, and sweet treats for visitors of all ages.
